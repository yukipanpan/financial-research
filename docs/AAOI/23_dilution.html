AAOI — Capital-Raising & Dilution Waterfall (2023 – Q1 2026)

Applied Optoelectronics, Inc. | CIK 0001158114 | Nasdaq: AAOI Sources: SEC EDGAR 424B5 prospectus supplements, 8-Ks (Items 1.01/2.03), FY2023/FY2024/FY2025 10-Ks, Q1-2026 10-Q, S-8 fee tables. Legend: ★ = filing fact (cited) · ◆ = computed by analyst.

0. Key takeaway

AAOI ran an aggressive, near-continuous equity-issuance machine from 2023–Q1 2026, primarily through ATM (at-the-market) programs sold by Raymond James (later joined by Needham), supplemented by one registered-direct deal and a convertible-note chain. Shares outstanding nearly tripled in two years (28.6M YE2022 → 79.0M Q1-2026). The single largest dilution wave was 2025, when the company sold **23.7M ATM shares for ~519Mnet * *whilethestockre − ratedfrom 13 to ~$30 on the AI/Amazon datacenter thesis.

  • Exercise price: $23.6956 per share | Term: 10 years
  • Vesting: 1,324,233 shares exercisable at issuance; remaining 6,621,166 vest over 10 years tied to Amazon (+ affiliates) purchasing $4 billion of AAOI product.

  • ◆ Potential dilution: up to 7,945,399 shares = ~10.1% of Q1-2026 shares (78.97M); near-term (vested) tranche 1,324,233 ≈ 1.7%.

7. Narrative summary

Total capital raised 2023–Q1 2026 (equity + new convert cash):

  • ATM equity net proceeds: $69.06M (2023) + $112.60M (2024) + 519.40M(2025) = ** 701.1M** ◆
  • Registered direct (2024-12): $33.7M net ★
  • New cash from converts: ~76.2M(2026Notes, 2023)+ 30M new cash (2030 Notes, 2024) = **~$106M** ◆ (the rest of the convert issuances were refinancing exchanges, not new cash)
  • Grand total ≈ 840M * *◆ofwhich * * 734M was straight common-equity dilution and ~$106M was net new convert cash.

Average issuance price vs. current: Blended ATM realized price ◆ ≈ $8.84 (2023) → $15.13 (2024) → $24.16 (2025); the registered direct cleared $33.97 and the Dec-25 ATM tranche hit 29.51.Thecapital − raisingfollowedthestock′sAI/datacenterre − rate—thecompanysoldthebulkofdollars(2025′s 519M) into the strongest tape, a textbook opportunistic ATM cadence.

Total potential overhang: beyond the ~50.3M shares already issued 2023→Q1-2026, the remaining forward overhang is **~13.2M shares (~16.7% of basic)** — Amazon warrant 7.95M (largest), 2030 Notes if-converted ~2.89M, RSUs ~2.34M, plus 5.6M of S-8-registered plan capacity (partially issued). The 2030 Notes ($125M, 2.75%, $43.31 strike, unhedged) are the only convert left; both prior series are fully retired.

Dilution waterfall (one line): 28.6M (YE22) → +9.5M (2023 ATM+converts) → 38.1M → +11.3M (2024 ATM+RD+converts) → 49.4M → +25.6M (2025 ATM mega-raise) → 75.0M → +4.0M (Q1-26) → 79.0M basic → ~92.1M fully diluted.
