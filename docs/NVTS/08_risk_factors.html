08 — 风险因素深度（Item 1A）+ 法律诉讼 + 持续经营

NVTS（Navitas Semiconductor Corp，CIK 1821769）。本页 = 7 步深挖之「风险因素 / 法律 / 持续经营」专题，对 report.md §17 的大幅扩写。 标签：★ SEC 直接披露 · ◆ 公式计算 · ◇ 第三方/公司 IR · ▽ 推算。cross-check：✅ 吻合 · ⚠️ 有差异 · ❌ 无法验证。单位双写（million / billion）。 主源：FY2025 10-K Item 1A（行 272–545）、Item 3 Legal（行 546–547）、Note 15 Commitments（行 1572–1573）、Item 9A Controls（行 1616–1639）、KPMG/Baker Tilly 审计报告（行 743–791）；Q1'26 10-Q Note 14（行 531–536）、Item 1 Legal（行 708–709）。 交叉引用：换审计与事件流见 20_8k_events.md；剥离测试与底层经营亏损见 report.md §13 与 00_initial_impression.md Agent D。

0. 本页一句话

NVTS 的风险版图不是「会不会被起诉」（无任何重大诉讼 ★），而是 「转型能否在现金耗尽前接棒」叠加「供应链单点（TSMC 退出 GaN）」叠加「治理/内控刚从重大缺陷里爬出来」——而且公司在风险因素里亲手写下了一条针对 NVIDIA 暴涨行情的自省式免责声明，明示投资者「不要过度依赖此类公告」。这是本案最具信息量的一段披露。

2. Top 10 风险因素分组深挖（含原文引语）

R1 — 转型执行风险：「revenues… one year or more, if ever」🔴 致命级·已部分兑现

公司在风险摘要第一条即写明（★ 10-K 行 279/338）：

"Even if we are awarded a design win, expected revenues typically do not result for one year or more, if ever. Further, revenues from design wins may not materialize if our customer changes, cancels or delays the selection or if our customer's products are not successful."

正文进一步（行 339）把转型期的脆弱性挑明：

"After incurring significant design and development expenditures … a substantial period of time generally elapses before we may generate meaningful net sales relating to such product, if at all. This may be especially true as we reprioritize our business towards high-power markets and deprioritize our emphasis on mobile and consumer products markets."

  • 为何致命：这是「主动放弃正在萎缩但真实存在的手机/消费收入，去赌一个一年以上才可能、甚至永不变现的高功率收入」的官方表述。营收 FY2024→FY2025 −45%（$83.302 million → $45.916 million）、Q1'26 再 −39% ◆ —— 设计导入「if ever」已经从风险变成了正在发生的现实。✅ cross-check：与 report.md §6 营收行一致。

R2 — 客户 / 经销商集中：单一经销商 46%🔴 已被验证一次

★ 10-K 行 19（distributor 风险，专条标题）：

"We depend on a few key distributors and the loss of one or more of these distributors could have a material adverse effect … This is especially true following our announcement that we will prioritize high-power markets. As a result of this change in our business, we reduced the overall number of distributors that we work with and placed additional reliance on a few key distribution partners."

  • 量化（★ Note 12）：FY2025 ≥10% 客户仅 Distributor B（46%）+ Distributor C（11%）；FY2024 的 Distributor A（56%）于 2024 年底被终止，直接造成 $7.5 million 坏账 + $5.0 million 存货减值 + $1.7 million 研发报废 ≈ $14.2 million（约 $0.0142 billion） 一次性损失。
  • 风险因素自承的二阶效应：「the use of distributors means that we are subject to channel inventory fluctuations and reduced demand transparency in the distribution channel」（行 19 末）——即 sell-in 确认收入（发货即确认，见 report.md §10）叠加经销商集中，终端真实需求能见度极低。⚠️ 这是会计政策（sell-in）与集中度风险的耦合点。

R3 — 供应链单点 / TSMC 退出 GaN：「sole supplier … cease GaN production in July 2027」🔴 致命级·新增

这是 FY2025 新出现、且最具结构性的风险。★ 10-K 行 378–379：

"We have historically relied on a single third-party manufacturer (wafer foundry) to fabricate our GaN products, and on a separate, single wafer foundry to fabricate our SiC products. … For example, historically, we have relied on TSMC as our sole supplier of GaN wafers … On July 1, 2025, TSMC announced its intention to cease GaN production in July 2027."

缓解措施与残余不确定（行 382）：

"We have taken proactive steps to mitigate this risk, including committing to purchase buffer inventory from TSMC, expanding our collaboration with Powerchip … and in November 2025, we announced a long-term strategic partnership with GlobalFoundries … Despite these mitigation steps, there is no assurance that we will be able to successfully source sufficient quality or volume of GaN wafers at a cost or on a timeline that meets our requirements. … the transition may require us to modify our designs or manufacturing processes or to undertake extensive re-qualification efforts … We are continuing to evaluate the impact of this transition on our financial statements."

  • 三层风险：①buffer 库存可能买不够，或买多了被迫报废（"end with excess inventory that must be scrapped"）；②Powerchip/GlobalFoundries 上量延迟；③重新认证（re-qualification）的工程成本与时间。
  • ⚠️ 未量化：过渡成本、buffer 规模、重认证费用均「continuing to evaluate」——这是 01_synthesis.md 列出的悬而未决问题之一。
  • 正反两面：美国本土代工（GlobalFoundries GaN / X-Fab SiC）反被公司包装成「国家安全相关市场的竞争优势」（★ 行 224）—— 一个风险（TSMC 退出）被叙事转化成卖点。✅ 但风险因素本身的「no assurance」语气诚实。

R4 — 中国 / 出口管制 / 对外投资新规🟠 结构性

  • 暴露：中国占终端客户 47%（★ MD&A，FY2025）。
  • 新规（★ 行 447 专条）：

"The U.S. government has implemented new regulations, effective January 2, 2025, that establish an Outbound Investment Security Program which prohibits or requires notification of certain investments by U.S. companies in entities involved in … semiconductor … in China, Hong Kong and Macau."

  • 四条具体影响（行 447 子项）：投资限制（禁止投 GaN 器件制造类中国实体）、通知义务、竞争劣势（仅约束美企）、违规罚则（民事/刑事）。公司自评：「we do not believe this materially limits our ability to enter into or continue customary commercial relationships with Chinese suppliers and customers」但限制了在 GaN 器件制造领域的战略投资/合作。
  • 判断：当前不限商业买卖、限战略投资；但「the full impact … remains uncertain and could be material」。叠加 47% 中国终端，地缘是中长期悬顶。

R5 — 资本充足 / 稀释：「might not be available … if at all」🔴 致命级·命门

★ 10-K 行 302/455：

"Our working capital needs are difficult to predict. We may require additional capital … and this capital might not be available on acceptable terms, if at all."

行 460 进一步点明稀释的自我强化（financing → dilution → 更难融资）：

"Any such financing … could … create significant shareholder dilution, which may make it more difficult to obtain additional capital … We may not be able to obtain additional financing on favorable terms, if at all."

  • 为何是命门：$236.857 million（约 $0.237 billion）现金 100% 来自 2025 年 ~$200 million 增发（ATM $100M + PIPE $100M），经营每年净流出 $42.9 million（FY2025）。无造血、靠稀释续命（详见 report.md §9、20_8k_events.md 事件流）。按 Q1'26 年化烧钱 ~$67 million，~$221 million 现金可支撑 ~3 年 ▽——但前提是「if at all」不成真。✅ 现金桥跨表勾稽通过。

R6 — 内控 / 重大缺陷：风险因素 + Item 9A「fully remediated」+ 审计师未鉴证🔴 红旗·已部分缓和

风险因素（行 461）保留缺陷复发的可能：

"Maintaining effective internal controls is an ongoing process, and if material weaknesses were to be identified in the future and not timely remediated … we could be unable to produce timely and accurate financial statements …"

关键事实校正（对早期 Agent E 表述的修正）：FY2025 10-K 并非「保留缺陷」，而是明确宣告整改完成。★ 行 462 / Item 9A 行 1621：

"During 2025, we successfully completed the remediation of the material weaknesses … management concluded that our internal control over financial reporting was effective as of December 31, 2025, and that the previously identified material weaknesses have been fully remediated."

但——KPMG 审计报告明确未对内控发表意见（★ 行 764，与行 791 重复）：

"The Company is not required to have, nor were we engaged to perform, an audit of its internal control over financial reporting … Accordingly, we express no such opinion."

  • 解读（本案最值得警惕的披露质量点）：
    1. FY2025「内控有效 + 缺陷已全部整改」是 管理层单方声明，KPMG 未做 Section 404(b) 鉴证（10-K 封面 404(b) attestation 勾选项 行 58–59）。
    2. 时间线对照（详见 20_8k_events.md）：2025-04-02 换审计 8-K 白纸黑字写「These material weaknesses … have not been remediated as of the date of this Current Report on Form 8-K」（FY2024 缺陷，截至 2025 年 4 月仍未整改）★ → 到 FY2025 10-K（2026-02）变成「fully remediated」。9 个月内从「未整改」到「全部整改且有效」，且无独立审计师背书。
    3. 缓和点：FY2022/A、FY2023/A、FY2024/A 均无财务重述（无 Item 4.02），缺陷是「设计/流程」层面而非「数字错报」。
  • 结论：方向在改善，但「自评有效 + 审计师明确不发表意见」让可信度打折。🟡→🔴。

R7 — 公告驱动的股价波动（自省式免责）：「do not place undue reliance」🔴 信息量最高

这是本案最罕见、最诚实、信息量最大的一条风险因素——公司亲手为 2025-05 NVIDIA 消息引发的暴涨写了免责。★ 10-K 行 327（摘要）与行 533/537/538（正文，全文罕见地重复了两次「undue reliance」）：

"The market price of our common stock may be affected by announcements regarding the selection of our products or technologies by key customers or industry participants, even though these events may not reflect binding commitments or future revenues. This may be particularly true following the Company's announcement of a pivot towards high-power markets. Investors should not place undue reliance on such announcements as an indicator of our business prospects, future performance or otherwise …"

正文行 537 更直白：

"…such announcements may generate significant investor interest and may lead to temporary fluctuations in our stock price … However, the … business opportunities … may not constitute binding commitments or guaranteed revenue … there is no guarantee … that any customer will ultimately place orders for our products or that any such orders will be material in amount."

  • 直指对象：NVIDIA 800V HVDC「collaboration」（2025-05 8-K）。该 8-K 是 Item 8.01「其他事件」而非 1.01「重大合同」，且与一次 $50 million ATM 增发捆绑同日披露（详见 20_8k_events.md 解码）。
  • 意义：公司用法律语言自我警告「背书 ≠ 订单」。这一条本身就是 01_synthesis.md 论点 1 的核心证据——估值由公告驱动，而公告无约束力。

R8 — inverted domestic corporation：政府合同受限🟠 SPAC 遗留·结构性

★ 10-K 行 312/473–474：

"As a consequence of Legacy Navitas being treated as an inverted domestic corporation under the Homeland Security Act, the U.S. federal government and certain state and local governments may refrain from entering into contracts with it in the future, which could substantially decrease the value of our business …" "The Federal Acquisition Regulation ('FAR') prohibits U.S. federal government agencies from using appropriated … funds for contracts with … an 'inverted domestic corporation,' as defined in the Homeland Security Act at 6 U.S.C. § 395(b)."

  • 讽刺张力：NVTS 把「AI 数据中心 + 电网现代化」包装成「national security implications」并以美国本土代工为卖点（行 224）；但其自身的倒置公司地位反而可能令其拿不到美国政府合同。「If our business becomes heavily dependent upon revenues generated from U.S. federal government contracts」时才致命——目前政府合同非主力，故列结构性而非即期风险。

R9 — 股东维权（stockholder activism）🔴 已兑现

★ 10-K 行 320/505–509。风险因素本身写得很「教科书」，但它已经发生了：

"Our business and operations could be impacted by stockholder activism … this may create a significant distraction … may result in changes to our senior management team … In addition, such stockholder activism may cause significant fluctuations in the market value …"

  • 已兑现的事实（详见 20_8k_events.md）：2025-04-23 与董事 Ranbir Singh / SiCPower, LLC 的和解协议（★ 2025-04-29 8-K Item 1.01+5.02+7.01）：CEO Sheridan 被剥夺董事长（→ Hendrix），创始 COO/CTO Kinzer 辞去董事 + 高管职务，成立 由 Singh 任主席的 Executive Steering Committee 监督资本配置/费用/高管接班，附带标准 standstill。风险因素里的假设语气，对应的是一桩已签署的协议——这是 01_synthesis.md 论点 3 的支柱。

R10 — IP 诉讼（纯假设，无在审案件）🟡 风险因素级·非实际诉讼

★ 10-K 行 498/45–55。这是纯前瞻性风险因素，不指向任何已发生的诉讼：

"The semiconductor industry is characterized by frequent litigation regarding patent and other intellectual property rights, including by both competitors and so-called 'non-practicing entities.' We have received communications, and we expect to receive additional communications from time to time, that allege or imply that our products or technologies infringe … or that invite us to take a license …"

  • 不可编造案件：此处只有「收到过质疑函/邀请授权」的一般性表述，未点名任何原告/案号/诉讼。✅ 与 Item 3 / Note 15「无重大诉讼」一致（见 §3）。
  • 正面信号：2024 年 10 月与 Infineon Technologies AG 达成广泛专利交叉授权（★ 行 253）——反映 IP 组合（300+ 专利）有谈判价值，而非被动挨告。

附：其他已披露但非 Top10 的风险（备查）

  • 网络安全（行 19 子节）：「In 2024 we experienced at least two attempted cyber attacks … one of which resulted in unauthorized access to non-critical systems」——已发生但未重大。
  • 税务 / NOL（行 481）：截至 2025-12-31，美国联邦 NOL 结转 ~$334.8 million（约 $0.335 billion）；Section 382 所有权变更（2025 年大额增发）可能限制 NOL 使用，公司「have not yet determined any resulting limitations」⚠️。
  • 应收账款 DCF 模型（行 17）：用折现现金流模型估应收回收，假设偏差有风险——与 FY2024 经销商终止坏账呼应。

3. 法律诉讼：无任何重大诉讼 ✅（不可编造）

Item 3 Legal Proceedings（★ 10-K 行 546–547）通过引用并入 Note 15：

"The information required by this item is incorporated by reference from 'Legal proceedings and contingencies,' included in Note 15 – 'Commitments and Contingencies'."

Note 15 原文（★ 10-K 行 1572–1573）：

"From time to time in the ordinary course of business, the Company may become involved in lawsuits … The Company makes a provision for a liability when it is both probable that a liability has been incurred and the amount of the loss can be reasonably estimated. The Company is not currently subject to any pending actions or regulatory proceedings that either individually or in the aggregate are expected to have a material impact on its consolidated financial statements."

Q1'26 10-Q 复核（★ Item 1 行 708–709 + Note 14 行 535–536）：

"We are currently not a party to any material legal proceedings." / "The Company is not currently subject to any pending actions or regulatory proceedings that … are expected to have a material impact …"

诉讼类型 是否披露 来源
证券集体诉讼（securities class action） ❌ 无 ★ Item 3 / Note 15 / Q1'26 Note 14
股东派生诉讼（derivative suit） ❌ 无 同上
专利 / IP 侵权诉讼（实际案件） ❌ 无（仅有一般性「收到质疑函」风险表述） ★ 行 45–55、498
监管调查（SEC/regulatory proceeding） ❌ 无 ★ Note 15 / Q1'26 Note 14

⚠️ 研究纪律提示：本案不存在任何已立案的诉讼。任何把 NVTS 与「证券欺诈集体诉讼」「派生诉讼」挂钩的叙述均为杜撰——IP-litigation 语言仅是行业通用风险因素。✅ 已在 FY2025 10-K + Q1'26 10-Q 两处独立确认。

4. 持续经营（Going Concern）：明确无疑虑 ✅

  • 全文检索：FY2025 10-K 与 Q1'26 10-Q 中**「going concern」「substantial doubt」零命中** ★。
  • KPMG / Baker Tilly 审计意见（★ 行 743–791）：标准无保留意见，无 going-concern 段落、无强调事项。审计仅就财务报表发表意见，明确不就内控发表意见（见 R6）。
  • 支撑事实：现金 $236.857 million（约 $0.237 billion）、零债务（仅微量融资租赁，FY2025 付息 $29 thousand）、~3 年跑道 ▽。
持续经营自检（为何"无疑虑"成立）
  现金 $236.9M ───┐
  零债务         ─┤──→ 即便每年烧 $43–67M，仍有 ~3 年缓冲 → 无 12 个月内违约/枯竭风险
  ~3 年跑道 ▽    ─┘     ∴ 审计师与管理层均未提 going concern ✅
  ⚠️ 但"无 going-concern"≠"安全"：跑道是稀释买来的，营收若继续 −40%/年则跑道缩短

辩证结论：going-concern 可以作为「可设置一边」的事项（详见 01_synthesis.md §「可设置一边」），但不可解读为「无生存风险」——它只说明「未来 12 个月不会枯竭」，不解决「3 年后靠什么」。
